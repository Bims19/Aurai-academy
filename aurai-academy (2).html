No technical background is required for the Newbie Track or Post-Grad Starter programme. Aurai Academy is designed for audit professionals, compliance officers, risk managers, and legal professionals who want to harness AI — not build it. Our curriculum starts with AI literacy fundamentals and progresses to practical agent deployment at your own pace.
The Agent Library is a curated collection of 300+ purpose-built AI agents, each specialised for a specific audit, compliance, or governance function. Access is provided through the Aurai platform portal and is included in all Practitioner, Team, and Enterprise subscriptions. Agents are browser-based — no installation or coding required. The library is continuously expanded, with new agents released monthly based on emerging regulatory requirements and community input.
The Newbie Track is a self-paced, individual learning programme designed for professionals beginning their AI audit journey. Enterprise Training is a bespoke, organisation-wide engagement delivered by Aurai faculty, with a custom curriculum, dedicated programme manager, and outcomes tied to your organisation's specific compliance obligations and business objectives. Enterprise Training typically involves teams of 15 or more and includes executive sponsorship frameworks, ROI tracking, and white-glove delivery.
Aurai Academy covers a comprehensive range of global frameworks including HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 42001 (AI Management Systems), NIST AI RMF, SOC 2, EU AI Act, CCPA, PCI DSS, and sector-specific regulations across financial services, healthcare, and public sector. Our framework coverage is continuously updated as regulations evolve, and enterprise clients can request custom modules aligned to jurisdiction-specific requirements.
The Certified AI Auditor and Intelligence Deployment (C‑AID) is Aurai Academy's flagship professional credential. It validates your ability to design, execute, and report on AI audits using intelligent automation. C‑AID is recognised by our network of employer partners across financial services, technology, healthcare, and consulting sectors globally. It signals both conceptual mastery and practical capability with AI audit tools and agent frameworks.
White-label partners receive a fully branded version of the Aurai platform — including your logo, colour scheme, domain, and custom report templates — powered by our underlying agent infrastructure. You retain full client ownership and billing relationships. Our team handles onboarding, technical setup, and ongoing platform updates. Partners receive access to the full agent library, co-branded certification, and dedicated support. The process from agreement to live platform is typically 10–15 business days.
The Practitioner Subscription is ongoing and self-paced, designed to be integrated alongside your existing professional responsibilities. Most practitioners complete the core curriculum within 6–12 weeks at 5–8 hours per week. Live cohort sessions run monthly, and you can join any cohort cycle.
